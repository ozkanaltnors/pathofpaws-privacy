PathOfPaws genel kitleye yonelik bir oyundur. Uygulama, bilerek cocuklardan kisisel veri toplamak amaciyla tasarlanmamistir.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the PathOfPaws mobile game handles user data.

This policy is intended for the Google Play version of the app. By using the app, you agree to the data handling practices described in this policy.

1. Developer Information

  • App name: PathOfPaws
  • Developer / publisher: Gepettousta
  • Contact email: ozkanaltnors@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect and Process

PathOfPaws processes limited data to provide gameplay features and support monetization and purchase functionality.

Data stored directly by the app:

  • game progress
  • unlocked animals and themes
  • level information
  • in-game currencies
  • settings and preferences

Data that may be processed through third-party services:

  • Unity Ads for advertising
  • Google Play Billing / Unity IAP for in-app purchases
  • advertising ID (Advertising ID / AD_ID)
  • device and network information
  • app interaction data
  • purchase status / transaction details

3. Data We Do Not Collect

PathOfPaws does not request or access:

  • location
  • camera
  • microphone
  • contacts
  • personal photo gallery content

4. How We Use Data

  • operate the game properly
  • save game progress
  • process in-app purchases
  • display ads and measure ad performance
  • analyze crashes, bugs, and performance issues
  • reduce fraud and abuse risks

5. Advertising

The app may display ads. Advertising providers may process device identifiers and technical data for ad delivery and measurement.

  • Advertising platform used: Unity Ads
  • Unity Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

6. In-App Purchases

The app may offer in-app purchases through Google Play. Payments are processed directly by Google Play. The app does not directly collect or store your credit card or payment card information.

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Data Retention

Gameplay progress and settings may remain stored on the device until the user removes the app, clears the app data, or resets the game state.

8. Data Deletion

Users can delete locally stored gameplay data by:

  • uninstalling the app
  • clearing the app's storage/data from device settings
  • using any in-app reset options if available

9. Children's Privacy

PathOfPaws is a general audience game. It is not designed to knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to help protect user data. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest published version is the current version, and the update date is shown at the top of this page.

12. Contact

  • Contact email: ozkanaltnors@gmail.com
